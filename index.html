Our Story

Once upon a time, in the bustling town of Shikrapur, there was a beautiful hotel called the Royal Palaace. The hotel was owned by a man named Sunil Sandbhor, who had always dreamt of opening a hotel that would offer the best possible experience to its guests.

As the years went by, the Royal Palaace continued to flourish, and Sunil's dedication and hard work paid off in spades. The hotel became a beacon of hospitality in Shikrapur, and Sunil became a respected member of the community.
